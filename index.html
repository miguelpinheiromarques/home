Biography

About me

Miguel is one of the most renowned Portuguese mastering engineers. He started his path in audio making records at Estúdios Sá da Bandeira, but soon found his passion in mastering which led him to pursue this craft as a career more than a decade ago. Since then he has worked with some of the biggest artists in Portugal and also with new emerging acts, mastering hundreds of releases for both major and independent labels, released nationally and internationally across all music genres.
